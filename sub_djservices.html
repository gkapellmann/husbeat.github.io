Group Workshops

Learn the fundamentals of DJing and get your hands on the tools!

This is a fun and informative course which is perfect for beginners who are curious about the world of DJing. You'll learn the basic skills and techniques needed to seamlessly mix tracks, and gain a deeper understanding of professional DJ equipment.

In this course you will:

  • Discover the fundamentals of beatmatching and mixing
  • Explore different DJ software and hardware setups
  • Get hands-on experience with professional DJ decks and digital controllers

This course is perfect for:

  • Music enthusiasts who want to learn how to DJ
  • Individuals looking for a new creative hobby
  • Anyone interested in exploring the art of music mixing

No prior experience is necessary!
